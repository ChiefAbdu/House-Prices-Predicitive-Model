
California Housing Dataset - Data Dictionary

Median_Income_10kUSD: Median income in tens of thousands of USD.
Median_House_Age_Years: Median age of houses in the block.
Average_Rooms: Average number of rooms per household.
Average_Bedrooms: Average number of bedrooms per household.
Population: Total population of the block.
Average_Occupancy: Average number of occupants per household.
Latitude: Geographic latitude.
Longitude: Geographic longitude.
Median_House_Value_100kUSD: Median house value in hundreds of thousands of USD.
MedHouseVal_USD: Median house value in U.S. Dollars.
